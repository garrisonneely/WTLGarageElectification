# Garage Electrification Subcommittee Charter

## 1. Name
**Garage Electrification Subcommittee** (hereinafter referred to as "the Subcommittee").

## 2. Purpose & Mission
The Subcommittee is established by the Board of Directors ("the Board") to research, evaluate, and recommend solutions for electric vehicle (EV) charging infrastructure within the Watertower Lofts garage. 

The Subcommitteeâ€™s primary mission is to develop a phased, scalable plan that enables residents to charge EVs safely and reliably, adhering to the principles of:
*   **Safety:** Prioritizing code compliance and eliminating hazards (e.g., extension cords).
*   **Fairness:** Ensuring equitable access and clear cost-sharing models.
*   **Scalability:** Designing solutions that accommodate future demand.

## 3. Scope of Responsibilities
The Subcommittee is advisory in nature and is authorized to:
*   **Assess Current State:** Document existing electrical electrical infrastructure, capacity constraints, and current charging behaviors.
*   **Coordinate Research:** Work through the Property Management company to engage with the utility provider (Xcel Energy) and qualified electricians/engineers.
*   **Develop Options:** Create technical and policy recommendations for:
    *   Immediate safety improvements (Phase 0).
    *   Intermediate 120V "drip charging" solutions (Phase 2).
    *   Long-term Level 2 (L2) charging programs (Phase 3).
*   **Draft Policies:** Propose rules regarding usage, billing, installation standards, and "right to charge" procedures for Board review.

**Out of Scope:**
*   The Subcommittee **does not** have the authority to sign contracts, authorize spending, or commit the HOA to financial obligations without Board approval.
*   The Subcommittee should not communicate directly with vendors or utilities as the "client" without coordination with Property Management.

## 4. Membership
*   **Composition:** The Subcommittee shall consist of at least [Number] and no more than [Number] members, appointed by the Board.
*   **Eligibility:** Membership is open to all owners in good standing. 
*   **Chairperson:** A Chairperson shall be selected by [the Board / the Subcommittee members] to organize meetings and act as the primary liaison to the Board.

## 5. Meetings & Reporting
*   **Frequency:** The Subcommittee will meet as needed to progress through the phases of the plan.
*   **Reporting:** The Subcommittee will provide regular status updates to the Board (e.g., monthly or quarterly) and present formal recommendations for a vote at Board meetings.
*   **Minutes:** Brief minutes or summaries of determinations should be kept for transparency.

## 6. Review & Dissolution
This charter shall be reviewed annually. The Subcommittee may be dissolved by the Board upon completion of its mission (implementation of a permanent EV solution) or at the Board's discretion.
